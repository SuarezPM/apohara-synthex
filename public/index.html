HONESTY · WHAT WE DO NOT CLAIM

The pitch is honesty — so it applies to us too.

✕RFC 3161 proves when, not truth. The timestamp proves the bytes existed and are unchanged — not that the content is correct.
✕The prefilter covers text/HTML injection, not visual VPI. 28 heuristic rules (aligned with SkillFortify) — not a formal proof, not screenshot-based attacks.
✕5 of 6 Bright Data APIs verified live, not 6. Crawl is implemented but pending a Crawl dataset_id — we don't claim it works until it's verified.
✕The Risk Score is an internal estimate. A deterministic heuristic with its formula printed on the PDF — not a Munich Re rating or a broker quote.
✕OTel export and Cognee memory are opt-in. The public endpoint keeps Cognee off to control cost; the rate-limit is best-effort.

Prior art, cited not claimed: the INV-15 formal-safety paper (Apohara Context_Forge) and the papers KVCOMM · MemArt · SkillFortify ground the design — they are not part of this scraping pipeline.
